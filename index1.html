About

What we do

Your food recipe website project is designed to provide a user-friendly platform for users to search, categorize, and explore various recipes based on ingredients, cuisine type, dietary preferences, and meal planning options. With a focus on frontend technologies like HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, the project integrates with APIs, such as Spoonacular, to fetch real-time recipe data, making the website dynamic and interactive.

projects

What we did?

1. Recipe Categorization: Users can browse recipes organized by different categories, such as cuisine type (e.g., Indian, Italian) and dietary preferences (e.g., vegetarian, non-vegetarian). This allows for a personalized browsing experience, making it easier to find relevant recipes.

2. User Interactions: Through search functionality and interactive recipe cards, users can filter results based on ingredients or cuisine preferences. Clicking on a recipe provides a detailed view on a new page, displaying the recipe’s ingredients, preparation instructions, and images.

3. Meal planning helps users organize their meals by selecting recipes for specific days and times, making it easy to plan weekly.
